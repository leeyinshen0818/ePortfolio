# Olist Business Intelligence Project

## Overview

This project builds a Business Intelligence dataset and dashboard from Olist e-commerce customer, order, and payment data. The workflow combines source datasets, prepares analytical fields, and presents business results through Power BI.

## Objectives

- Integrate customer, order, and payment datasets.
- Prepare delivery, revenue, payment, and order-status measures.
- Build a reusable data-transformation workflow.
- Create a dashboard for business analysis.

## Tools and Technologies

- Alteryx Designer workflow
- Power BI
- CSV data
- Business Intelligence modelling and reporting
