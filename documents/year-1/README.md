# 🎓 Year 1

Welcome to my first year in the Bachelor of Computer Science (Data Engineering) programme at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM).

Year 1 focused on developing a foundation in programming, computer systems, software design, mathematical thinking, and information technology.

## 📚 Academic Overview

During this year, I developed introductory knowledge in:

- C++ programming and problem solving
- Digital logic and computer architecture
- Human-computer interaction
- System analysis and design
- Information systems and technology

## 📝 Reflection

Year 1 introduced me to both the theoretical and practical sides of computing. Programming exercises strengthened my logical thinking, while hardware and system-related subjects helped me understand how software connects with computer components and organisational needs.

Group assignments and projects also improved my communication, teamwork, and time-management skills. This year provided the foundation needed for more advanced work in software development, databases, and data engineering.
