## What I Did

I documented my observations and learning from the visit.

To complete this assignment, I focused on reviewing the available information, organising the main points, and presenting them through the required academic evidence. I reviewed the available files and outputs together so that the implementation, written explanation, and supporting evidence remained connected to the task objectives. Where the evidence did not clearly describe a personal contribution, I kept the description limited to the work that can be verified from this folder.

## Key Learning Outcomes

I gained awareness of how large organisations use technology and information systems.

The work also strengthened my understanding of technology research, information systems, professional communication, teamwork, and the connection between technology and organisational needs. I became more aware that a technical result should be supported by a clear process, suitable evidence, and an explanation of why the selected approach was appropriate. This will help me approach related tasks more systematically in future coursework.

## Reflection

The visit made the subject more practical by showing technology in an industry setting.

The most important challenge was selecting relevant information and presenting technical ideas clearly without making the final material too crowded. Working through this required me to check the task in smaller stages and compare the result with the available requirements or evidence. This process improved my patience and made me more careful about verifying technical work before presenting it.

Overall, this assignment contributed to my development by combining practical work with documentation and reflection. It showed me which parts I can now complete with more confidence and which areas still require further practice. The experience will be useful when I work on larger projects that demand clearer organisation, stronger testing, and more independent technical decisions.

## Possible Improvements

A useful next step would be to add visit date, agenda, and a clearer caption for the image.

In a future version, I would also focus on stronger source referencing, clearer visual hierarchy, and a more explicit summary of individual contribution. These additions would make the work easier to understand, reproduce, and evaluate while providing stronger evidence of the development process.
